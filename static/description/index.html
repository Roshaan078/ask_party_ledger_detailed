Overview

The Detailed Partner Ledger module provides a comprehensive ledger report for any partner (customer or vendor) in Odoo. After installation, it can be accessed via PDF Reports in the Accounting module.

Select the desired date range, journals, and partner(s) to generate a report including all invoice lines, products, debit/credit values, and running balances.

Generating the Report

Once all filters are applied, click the Print button. A PDF file will be generated containing detailed entries per partner, including opening balances and product lines.

Invoice Lines and Products

The report includes all invoices with their products listed line by line. Debit and credit amounts are calculated correctly based on invoice type, and opening balances are shown for the selected date range.
